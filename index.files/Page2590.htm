V. The rules of submission of the scientific articles of authors

 

The list of accompanying documents

(formal examination)

· the recommendation of educational, scientific or other organization
with the indication of the scientific journal or the newspaper and section,
in which publication of the scientific article is supposed – 1 pts.;

· the reference about authors with the indication of a surname, a name and a middle initials for each author,
a place of work and post, scientific degree and academic status with the years of assignment and award
(HEI, which was graduated by the author and years of the finished),
quantity of printing works of the author(s) and area of scientific interests of “The NCO(E) SIO "ACNS"” – 1 pts.;

· the expert judgment about possibility of publication of the scientific article in the open press – 1 pts.;

· the accompanying letter of any form
(only for the authors, who aren't the staff of divisions of “The NCO(E) SIO "ACNS"”) – 1 pts.

 

Publication process

(examination in essence)

· one or several scientific articles (for examination in essence) representing by the author (authors)
in an editorial board of the chosen section of a certain scientific journal or the newspaper
with accompanying documents (for formal examination);

· the scientific article of the author in section of the scientific journal or the newspaper have to be issued
it agrees operating (R)ISO, GOST and USCD for scientific editions
for the purpose of publication in Publishing house of “The NCO(E) SIO "ACNS"”;

· editorial board (editor) of a certain section of the scientific journal or newspaper
carries out formal expertise and examination on the substance of the scientific article,
additional reviewing (if necessary or absence of the review),
editing (the author and the editor will agree on contents of the scientific article in three readings and two editings),
includes the scientific article in the approved plan of the edition of issue(№)of the scientific journal or the newspaper;

· previously imposed issues (numbers) materials of scientific journals or newspapers
are presented to Publishing house of “The NCO(E) SIO "ACNS"”
in the form of the *.doc and *.rtf format file on an electronic medium of information,
and also (or) in the form of listing on the printer on paper an A4 format (210х297 mm);

· issues (numbers) materials of scientific journals or newspapers are officially published and duplicated
on the global network Internet (WWW) (the electronic publication),
on an optical data carrier of CD ROM/DVD-ROM
with a firm label and holographic protection (the publication in the form of the electronic book in the *.pdf format)
and also on standard (glossy) paper data carrier of information of the A4 format
with a firm label and protection (temporary no publication in the form of the standard book with cover).
