Academy structure

“The noncommercial organization (establishment) (NCO(E))
“The state international organization (SIO)
"Academy of cognitive
natural sciences"”” (“SIO "ACNS"”)
includes administrative-scientific divisions (I),
scientific-organizational divisions (II),
administrative-economy divisions (III),
scientific-research and education
management divisions (IV),
fundamental scientific researches management divisions
of “SIO "ACNS"” –
branches and sections (departments) (IV.A),
applied scientific researches management divisions
of “SIO "ACNS"” –
directions and sections (departments) (IV.B),
divisions of “SF "SFA CMT"
named after N.A. Prokopenko” (additional) (V),
scientific-historical divisions
of “SIO "ACNS"” (additional) (VI),
scientific-publical divisions
of “SIO "ACNS"” (additional) (VII)
divisions of “SRI "SFA CMT"
of "RA(N)S" named after V.N. Veniaminov”
(additional) (VIII)
and other divisions.
