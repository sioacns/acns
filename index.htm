Modern painting, architecture,
design and art

 

The “Suprematism” – a modern
direction in painting, which
allows to generate the difficult
geometrical figure on the plane
by means of a set of the simple
geometrical figures
(К. Malevich).

 

The “Black square” –
concentration
of the unidentified in depth
of two measurements
(К. Malevich).

The “Red square” –
a projection
of the peasant woman in depth
of two measurements
(К. Malevich).

 

The “Cognitive volumetric suprematism”
(also “Cognitive spherism”) –
 the new direction in modern painting,
architecture, design and art
which allows to generate
the volumetric difficult graphic
picture by means of a set
of the simple flat and volumetric
geometrical figures in the Decart
three-dimensional system of coordinates
(the method of eight points
and eight contours).

(Vetrov A.N.).

 

The “Cognitive black sphere” – concentration of the ideal non-linear
and non-unique unidentified
in environment of three measurements
(Vetrov A.N.).

 

The “Cognitive red sphere” –
ideal non-linear and non-unique
model of “The Sun”
in environment of three measurements
(Vetrov A.N.).
