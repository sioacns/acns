5. The directions of scientific researches
of “SIO "ACNS"”

Is engaged in the (microscopic) analysis
and increase of efficiency
of the adaptive information-
educational environments functioning;

develops and modernizes the aggregate
of cognitive modeling technology
for the (microscopic) system analysis
of information-educational environments;

develops and modernizes the cognitive models
of subject and means of training structures,
and also is engaged in creation and modernization
of architecture of adaptive automated
means of training
based on parametrical cognitive models.
 

Is engaged in the (microscopic) financial analysis
and increase of efficiency
of (credit) organization functioning;

develops and modernizes the aggregate
of cognitive modeling technology
for the (microscopic) financial analysis
of organization structures
of enterprises and (credit) organizations;

develops and modernizes the cognitive models structures
for the vertical, horizontal and trend
(microscopic) financial analysis
of (credit) organizations based on
primary registers of financial
and accounting reports (documents),
and also is engaged in creation and modernization
of architecture of automated means
of the financial analysis and audit
based on parametrical cognitive models.

Is engaged in “Theoretical mechanics”,
“Physics”, “Chemistry”,
“Molecular Biology” and “Medicine”,
“The (microscopic) analysis and increase
of efficiency of difficult
objects, processes and phenomena functioning”;

develops and modernizes the aggregate
of cognitive modeling technology
for the (microscopic) complex analysis
of difficult objects, processes and phenomena;

creates and modernizes cognitive models
for the (microscopic) complex analysis
based on cognitive cycle, cognitive disc,
cognitive cylinder, cognitive cone,
cognitive spheres, and also
is engaged in creation and modernization
of architecture of automation means
for the (microscopic) complex analysis based on
parametrical cognitive models block.
