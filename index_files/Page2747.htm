includes the information technologies,
the informatisation
of information environments
of educational organizations,
architecture of adaptive
means of training,
“(Cognitive) computer science”,
“(Cognitive) psychology”,
“(Cognitive) linguistics”,
“Economics theory”,
“The financial analysis, money and credit”,
“Theoretical mechanics”,
“Physics”, “Chemistry” and “Molecular biology”.

The note:

1. The scientific research centres are created in territory of RF and the foreign countries
only after the sanction and reception of the license
of the author of the unique cognitive modeling technology
(for the microscopic system, financial and complex analysis),
The Government of RF, The Russian author's society
(as agreed with The Government of the foreign country for non-residents).

2. The scientific research institutes are accepted in structure
of “The state international organization "Academy of cognitive natural sciences"”
only after the sanction and reception of the license
of the author of unique cognitive modeling technology
(for the microscopic system, financial and complex analysis),
The Governments of RF, The Russian author's society
(as agreed with The Government of the foreign country for non-residents).

3. The settlement of disagreements and registration of the specified organizations is carried out.
